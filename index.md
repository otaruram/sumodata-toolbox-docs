## Why SumoData Toolbox?

Traditional AI coding assistants are chatty and token-hungry. SumoData Toolbox is different:

- **🎯 Focused**: Each tool does ONE thing well
- **⚡ Fast**: No conversation history, just instant results
- **💰 Efficient**: Minimalist prompts save your API quota
- **🛠️ Practical**: Built specifically for data professionals
